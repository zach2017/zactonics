Live Scenarios

Grocery retail operates on razor-thin margins in a high-velocity, high-complexity environment. A single product recall, an unexpected demand spike, a vendor delivery failure, or a wave of negative customer feedback can erode profitability within hours if the right decision-makers don't have the right information at the right time.

This tutorial demonstrates how Apache NiFi — combined with AI models, autonomous agents, and a real-time executive intelligence layer — transforms raw operational data from dozens of grocery store systems into clear, actionable insight.

Why Apache NiFi?

Guaranteed delivery, full provenance audit trail, back-pressure management for peak loads, and 300+ processors connecting to every system in the grocery stack — POS, ERP, WMS, CRM, IoT sensors, vendor EDI, and government recall APIs — without custom code.

Why AI Agents?

LLMs and specialized ML models transform NiFi event streams from raw data into executive-ready intelligence. Agents close the loop — not just surfacing insights but autonomously executing approved response playbooks: drafting recalls, adjusting inventory thresholds, routing tickets, and generating board-ready situation reports in seconds.

Executive Capabilities Enabled
Detect product safety recalls and coordinate store responses within 90 seconds
Identify sales velocity anomalies and trigger dynamic pricing interventions
Monitor vendor delivery performance and automatically escalate SLA breaches
Analyze customer feedback in real time to surface emerging quality issues
Receive AI-generated situation reports and ranked action plans
Deploy autonomous AI agents acting on approved playbooks without human bottlenecks
