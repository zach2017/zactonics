About the Gideon Generation Initiative

The Vision

We — the members of the Regional Conferences — are rising up to stand behind 300 Oakwood University students. In Judges 7, God chose 300 fully committed soldiers to win an impossible battle. These 300 students are our Gideon Generation — called, consecrated, and chosen.

The Platform

This website and companion mobile app allow church members to browse student profiles, register as monthly sponsors, connect with their student through staff-facilitated virtual meetings, and track academic progress in real time.

Privacy & Safety

All virtual meetings are facilitated by a Regional Conference staff member. Student privacy is protected at all times. Administrators manage all access levels.
