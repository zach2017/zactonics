How to use

• Drag a component to the canvas.

• Click a node to edit title, failure notes, and recovery steps.

• Shift+Click two nodes to create a connection.

• Click a line label to edit connection details.

• Saved automatically to local storage.

Tip: Shift+Click two nodes to connect.
