Failure simulation

When simulation mode is on, click a node to simulate a failure. Downstream nodes and connections will be marked as impacted.

How to use

• Drag a service from the palette to the canvas.

• Drag from any of a node's four anchor dots to another node to create a connection.

• Shift+Click two nodes also creates a connection.

• Click any node or line to edit details.

• Use failure templates to quickly model resiliency.

• Risk level colors show weak areas at a glance.

• Simulation mode shows blast radius through dependencies.
