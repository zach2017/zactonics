The recipe · Use it every time

Five ingredients. Every prompt.

If a prompt feels off, one of these is missing. The activities below already have CRAFT baked in — you just fill the blanks.

C
Context
Who you are, who it's for, what's going on.
R
Role
"Act as a copywriter / a CFO / my customer."
A
Ask
One specific thing you want. Not five.
F
Format
Email · table · 3 versions · 200 words.
T
Tone
Warm · blunt · formal — or paste a sample.

Refrigerator magnet · Pin this

Six DOs. Six DON'Ts.

✓ Do
  • ✓ Give Claude a role. "Act as a copywriter."
  • ✓ Show, don't tell. Paste examples of "good."
  • ✓ Specify the format. Length, structure, table.
  • ✓ Tell Claude what NOT to do.
  • ✓ New chat for a new topic.
  • ✓ Ask for 3 options when you can't decide.
✕ Don't
  • ✕ Ask open "what do you think?"
  • ✕ Assume Claude knows your brand.
  • ✕ Accept the first draft if it's off.
  • ✕ Paste anything sensitive — period.
  • ✕ Trust a stat or citation unverified.
  • ✕ Substitute for a lawyer / doctor / CPA.
