01 — Overview

Standing up a production Kafka cluster

This runbook documents the repeatable deployment of a 3-broker Amazon MSK cluster across three Availability Zones, provisioned entirely through CloudFormation. Every property is version-controlled, every step is idempotent, and the whole stack tears down with a single command.
