Frequently Asked Questions

What does the proof of concept phase look like?
We start by understanding your challenge, then build a focused, working prototype in 2–4 weeks. You'll be able to test it with real data and see concrete value before committing to a full build.
What AI technologies do you work with?
We work across the major platforms — Custom App Claude, OpenAI, Azure AI Services, as well as open-source tools like Ollama, PyTorch, FastText, and TensorFlow.js. We pick the right stack for your use case.
Can you build solutions that run on our own hardware?
Absolutely. We specialize in privacy-first, offline deployments using custom models on your internal infrastructure — no cloud dependency required.
What are AI Security Assessments?
We use frameworks like MITRE ATLAS and CRISP-ML(Q) to identify and mitigate AI-specific threats — poisoning, evasion, prompt injection — ensuring your AI systems are secure across their entire lifecycle.
How do I get started?
Reach out via the contact section below or email us directly. We'll schedule a discovery call to understand your goals and map out a plan — no commitment required.
