Chapter One
A place
made for you.

Imagine a campus where your faith is celebrated, your future is invested in, and your people already feel like family — that's Oakwood.

Located in Huntsville, Alabama, Oakwood University is a private, faith-rooted, historically Black college serving students who want more than a degree. We build the doctors, attorneys, founders, engineers, educators, ministers and creatives the world is waiting on — and we don't ask you to leave who you are at the gate.

Quick Answers

FAQ

Do I have to be Seventh-day Adventist to attend? +

No. Oakwood is rooted in the SDA Christian tradition, but we welcome students of all backgrounds who want a faith-conscious, community-rich college experience.

What if I'm undecided on my major? +

Welcome. Our advisors are pros at helping you explore your options through general education courses, interest surveys, and 1:1 conversations.

Is Oakwood actually affordable? +

Yes. Most students receive a combination of federal aid, institutional scholarships and work-study. Our financial aid team builds your package with you.

Can I visit before I apply? +

Absolutely — and you should. We host group visit days, individual tours, and virtual tours. Tap "Plan a campus visit" up top.

What's the vibe? For real. +

Family. Faith. Drive. You'll work hard, laugh harder, eat together, pray together, and graduate with people who'll text you for the rest of your life.
