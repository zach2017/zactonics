Stop 2 · Why Oakwood

Faith is a feature,
not a footnote.

"God First" isn't decoration — it's the daily rhythm of campus life. Vespers Friday nights. Mentors who'll pray with you. A community that won't let you fall.

Oakwood is a Seventh-day Adventist HBCU in Huntsville, Alabama. Founded in 1896. Built for students who refuse to leave their faith at the door — and who graduate ready to serve.
